## Notes
--------

Design problems

1. The killer reason for local split screen has not been found. Nostalgia is not a good enough
reason on its own.
2. Core tension not confirmed; Timing/Sequencing (Kingdom) or Space/Resources (Dungeon of the Endless)?
	NB: Read the core_tensions.txt for more insight intot this
3. Shared village vs separate village sin co-op. Separate villages give stronger individual identity
but complicate the shared world design.
	NB: One solution to this could be in transferable characters. Each player would have their own
	world and village on their PC. But if a friend comes over for split-screen, they can bring their
	own charcater form their own world and contribute and progress in their friends world, before
	returning back to their own. One advantage to this is if player choices create distinct
	possibilites; player 1's choices lead to abundant mithril but no coal. Player 2's, lead to the
	opposite. Joining each other's worlds gives access to the other (Pokemon effect).
4. Visual identity / art style not yet found. Death's Door and Pyre are starting points.


technical / Scope Problems

1. The Fable NPC reacctivity is ambitious and must be th elast thing to be built.
2. Without strict design boundaries, systems development will expand to fill any available dev time.
3. How slow should village progressio be? Feel this out.
