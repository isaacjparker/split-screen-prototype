# core_vision
Project : Split Screen Prototype
Created : 2026-03-23
====================

## Notes
--------

Working game title: Nobility

A cooperative local multiplayer action RPG with village building, procedural maps, and deep 
player expression. The game is fundamentally about the player as author; of their character,
their village, their strategy, and the emergent story created.

Replayability and identity come from player choices, not handcrafted content. Two players
are able to have very different experiences and see themselves reflected in their worlds
in different ways, without major variations in content.


Features:
	Top-down 3D action RPG
	Accessible controls and gameplay
	Low-poly, stylised, readable visual language
	1-4 player local split screen co-op
	Shared player progression
	Procedural explorable maps
	"Village as player build"
	Player expression through village and character build

Mechanics:
	Melee, Ranged, Magic combat
	Village building
	NPC recruitment
	Player XP
	Single resource
